Memory Match

A small browser game for practicing short-term memory. Flip two cards at a time, remember their positions, and try to match all pairs with fewer moves.

Setup

Choose a level and a card set.

Rules:
Pick two cards. Matching cards stay open. Non-matching cards flip back, so you have to remember where they were.

Design notes

This one-file version uses a simple layout instead of a large setup. The left side is for setup, and the right side is for playing. The card grid uses similarity and proximity, and the open cards use contrast so the player can quickly see what changed.

Persona 1: Maya

Maya wants a calm game that is easy to understand during study breaks, so the design avoids too many controls.

Persona 2: Leo

Leo likes replaying for a better score, so the game keeps moves, time, matches, and best score visible.
